My Subject: Realme Smartphones

Introduction

Realme (stylized as realme) is a Shenzhen-based Chinese smartphone manufacturer. The brand was officially established on May 4, 2018 (National Youth Day in China) by Sky Li (Li Bingzhong), who was previously the Vice-President of Oppo Electronics. Realme also produces a wide variety of other products such as earphones, fitness bands and bags. It is the brand ambassador for Ayushmann Khurrana from Realme XT and Salman Khan from Realme 6 and 6 Pro.
